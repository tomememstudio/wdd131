Marvy Accessories

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Marvy Accessories - A jewelry brand focused on elegant, affordable and stylish accessories for modern women.

This name was chosen beacuse it is the real brand identity and clearly communicates beauty, style, and femininity

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an informational and promotional platform for Marvy Accessories. It will showcase jewelry collections, tell the brand story, and provide contact information for customers. The site will also include interactive JavaScript features such as dynamically displayed featured products and responsive user interactions.

Scenarios

  • What types of jewelry collections does Marvy Accessories offer?
  • How can I contact the brand or place an order?

Color Schema

The selected colors reflect elegance, luxury, and femininity:

  • Purple (#6a0dad) – Used for headings, accents, and branding elements.
  • Gold (#f2c94c) – Used for highlights, buttons, and decorative accents.
  • Soft Background (#faf7ff) – Used as the page background for a clean, elegant feel.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for headings to create a luxurious and elegant appearance.
  • Poppins – Used for body text for readability and modern design.
